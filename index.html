Stonewall Academy


School Hours

School begins at 9:10 a.m. Students may enter the building at 8:45 a.m. If students arrive tardy to school but before 9:20 a.m., they should report to class. Students arriving after 9:20 a.m. must report to the Attendance Office. School ends at 3:40 p.m. Everyone must be off campus by 4:05 p.m.
